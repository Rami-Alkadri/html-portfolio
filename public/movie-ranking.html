The Best Movies According to Rami

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

Watched this movie on Turkish Airlines. I thought it would be overrated, but was very much wrong. One of the best movies of all time

The Martian

A great film. I have zero complaints or reservations about this movie; it is spectacular from start to finish.

The Wolf of Wall Street

I don't care that this is every finance bro's favorite movie. I love Leonardo DiCaprio and his depiction of Jordan Belfort. This movie is wildly entertaining.
